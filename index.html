Understanding Telehealth Regulations in Florida

Florida law requires that out-of-state physicians providing telehealth services to patients within the state register with the Florida Department of Health or hold an active, unencumbered Florida medical license. Prescriptions for controlled substances via telehealth are highly regulated, and valid patient-provider relationships must be established prior to the dispensing of restricted medications.
